semantic roles

volitional actors: the thing doing the action, usually animate
ex: she threw the ball. -> she is the thrower
not an example: the ball dropped. -> the ball is the subject but not causing the drop

Role:Definition

Agent: The volitional causer of an event
ex: I love apples -> I

Experiencer: 
ex: the tornado destroyed the village -> village

Instrument:
ex: john kicked the ball with his foot -> with his foot

Beneficiary: the beneficiary of an event. (positive receiving?)
ex: john kicked the ball to me -> to me

Source:
ex: I came from Moscow -> from Moscow 

Goal:
ex: I wan to go to Yakahama, Japan. -> to Yakahama, Japan

Differences between adjuncts, arguments, and modifiers?
- arguments are required to make complete predicates
- adjunct is not required but adds more to the predicate
- modifiers are not required either
